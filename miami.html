The Ultimate Bachelor Party Weekend

Miami Edition

With beautiful white sand beaches, perfect weather, beautiful women and a thriving club scene, there is little not to like about Miami Beach for your bachelor party. This guide will tell you everything you need to do and see to experience your own Miami Vice

Things to Do
Golf

Tough to beat Golf in Florida. If you and the crew want to get a round or two in during your bachelor party then there are some solid options in Miami Beach:

Best Options

Miami Beach Golf Club offers a beautiful course right near downtown South Beach (most likely close to where your Airbnb/VRBO/hotel will be). For public golf, it doesn’t get much better in the area then Miami Golf Beach Club. Green fees range from $125 - $225 depending on time of the year and day of the week (winter months are more expensive). The club has a great restaurant and lunch menu for a solid meal before or after the round.

Normandy Shores Golf Club is a slightly cheaper option than Miami Beach Golf Club and still very close to South Beach. Greens fees fall between $95 - $125 depending on time of year.

Best Course Options

We-Ko-Pa Golf Club offers two beautiful 18 hole courses 30 mins outside of the entertainment district (most likely closer to where your Airbnb will be). The club offers some awesome 36 hole packages ranging from $125 - $395 depending on time of the year (winter months are more expensive). The club has a delicious lunch menu for in between rounds and a multiple beverage carts that are never too far away. If you have a crew that wants to play 36 this is tough to beat.

Troon North Golf Club is another 36 hole golf club located a bit further from downtown and a bit more pricey. Troon is consistently rated as top courses and public golf clubs in Arizona. Desert golf at it’s finest with beautiful views of the mountains as a backdrop make this tough to beat. The experience doesn’t stop on the course - the beautiful clubhouse offers world class dining and exceptional service. The crew can’t go wrong at Troon North.

Miami Shores Golf Club is located across the bride closer to downtown Miami. Greens fees at this public/private course range from $79-$109 depending on the time of year. This is great value for a group looking to get a round in during the bach party.

Job Skis

If the group is looking to be active then there is plenty to do on the water in Miami Beach - jet ski tours, parasailing, boat rentals and more. Flock can help you book all of these depending on what you desire

Best Options:

Extreme Jet Ski Tour offers a number of different options for the group - 90 minute guided tour, 90 minute rental, half day rental, & full day rental. Spend the day ripping around the beautiful beaches and bay with the crew to work off a hangover

Pool Party

Hard to find a better way to spend the day on a bachelor party in Miami Beach then a pool party. Beautiful girls, cabanas, and talented DJs is the perfect way to spend a Saturday on a bachelor party.

Best Options:

Fontainebleau: Home to the famous LIV nightclub, Fontainebleau is a Miami staple with a live DJ pool party from 1 PM to sunset every Saturday and Sunday. There are a number of options at different price points for food and beverage minimums if the crew wants to get a table/cabana.

Delano: Perfect location right in the heart of South Beach. The Delano Hotel has a Live DJ from 1PM to Sunset at it’s beautiful pool. You can’t go wrong with this option and it has some of the best deals in town for pool party bottle service.

Hyde: Hyde boasts a Vegas-like pool party right by the beach. This spot tends to be one of the pricier options but expect some top talent DJs and a big time party

Bars & Night Clubs

Miami Beach is known for it’s nightlife. The area is full of great clubs with table and bottle service for the crew that are packed almost every nighht of the week. There are plenty of options for a bachelor party to have a great time from North Miami Beach all the way down to the bottom of South Beach.

Some of the best clubs:

LIV is the most popular club in Miami Beach. Guaranteed to be packed, live DJs created an awesome rave and club atmosphere similar to Vegas. Purchase tickets in advance or table for quick access and bottle service.

WALL is known as the top club on Friday nights. The crew will need to buy tickets for ~$40 GA or a table ($1,500 minimum, $187 per guy). If you want to see top live DJ talent in the rave atmosphere that Miami Beach is known for then this is your spot.

If the crew wants something a little less clubby or maybe somewhere to hit before the clubs, head to Broken Shaker- an awesome outside bar that is more low key but still a great vibe.

Where to Eat

Breakfast

11th Street Diner - Good old fashioned delicious diner to saddle up and lick the wounds of the night before. 11th Street Diner is right in downtown South Beach and won’t let you down.

Front Porch Cafe - Breakfast served all day at this hip spot right in South Beach. Consistently rated highly on yelp, this is a can't miss.

The Social Club - Swanky but delicious brunch spot. Served everyday until 3 PM. A great place for a late breakfast and a little more hair on the dog

Dinner

Matador Room - Does the crew want a nice dinner to kick of the weekend? Look no further than Matador Room. Awesome bar & restaurant with indoor and outdoor seating and live DJ - the food is great (get the Ribeye), and if your going to the club Basement (highly recommended) it is in the same hotel so its a perfect place to kick off the night

Lario's on the Beach - If you go to Miami Beach and don’t have Cuban food, did you actually even go? Lario’s is a swank Cuban restaurant with great food and the perfect location - you can’t ask for much more

Late Night Food

Sweet Liberty Drinks & Supply Company - this spot has top notch cocktails but more importantly a great late night menu that features a burger large enough to feed a small family. This is the South Beach late-night staple - open Sunday to Thursday until 3 a.m., Fridays and Saturdays until 4 a.m.

Bodega South Beach - This is a late night must in Miami Beach. Sure it has delicious tacos, burritos, and late night mexican but a hidden bar in the back open until 5 am is what makes this the go to spot.

Pizza Bar - What’s late night drunk food without pizza? Pizza Bar is Miami Beach’s spot for a slice. Great place to crush some huge slices at the end of the night. Open Sunday to Wednesdays until 2 a.m., Thursday to Sundays until 5 a.m.

Where to Stay

Miami Beach is known for its high-end and stylish hotels. The best restaurants, bars, and clubs all tend to be in a hotels - which makes easy for a bachelor party. Though these will tend to be a higher price per head than alternative options, it’s hard to turn down the convenience of hotel staying in Miami Beach.

Fontainebleau: As we mentioned in the pool party section, Fontainebleau is a Miami Beach staple. The location isn’t perfect - it’s a cab right south to the heart of South Beach but Fontainebleau has a top notch pool party, the nightclub LIV, and very nice accomodations.

Delano is another great option with a perfect location right in the heart of South Beach. With a great pool party as well the Delano is hard to ignore if your crew is going the hotel route.

Mondarian is a great spot on the west side of the peninsula. Though not right in action of South Beach, Mondarian is a great option just a short cab ride away with an awesome pool and great amenities.

Though less common due to the prevalence of great hotels, a VRBO/Airbnb is a great way to go in Miami Beach. There are plenty of great condos available to book right on Ocean Drive in the heart of the beach, restaurants, bars and clubs. There are also a number of great houses in the center of the peninsula with roof decks, pools, etc for those bach parties that want to all be in the same place. Flock will help make sure you land the accommodations that is perfect for your crew

Where to Eat

Good food may be the most important key to a great bachelor party, and luckily Scottdale has plenty to offer for both big dinners and quick stops. Hopefully the crew likes TexMex!

Breakfast

Breakfast Club - Good old fashioned delicious diner to saddle up and lick the wounds of the night before. Breakfast Club is right in the Entertainment district and won’t let you down.

Los Favoritos Taco Shop - nothing fancy about this local taco chain, but if you are staying northeast of Old Town, we highly recommend hitting this place up for some breakfast burritos

Butters Pancakes & Café offers delicious breakfast, lunch, and dinner. A great spot to grub up and put a little hair on the dog. Pro tip - put your group on the waitlist via Yelp before you arrive so you can get right to it

Late Night Food

Beverly on Main is a hidden gem - not only is it an awesome gastropub with live DJ and great cocktails, it also has an open kitchen until 2 am on Friday and Saturday night. Grub out on the “midnight brunch” menu after a long day of partying

Mr. Mesquite Street Tacos is the go to spot for late night tacos outside the clubs of the Entertainment District. Open to 3 AM, it’s tough to go wrong with anything on this delicious menu at this quick stop spot.

Joe's New York Pizza - What’s drunk food without pizza? Open to the 3:30AM and located right outside the bars in the entertainment district, this place won’t let you down when your looking for that post-club bite.

Where to Stay

A VRBO/Airbnb is definitely the way to go for Scottsdale. There is plenty of great inventory just outside of Old Town for very affordable prices. The market has adapted to this being a great spot for bachelor parties - if you plan in advance you are guaranteed to find a well-priced option with a pool and plenty of beds

Neighborhoods

You basically want to stay as close to Old Town as possible. Scottsdale is tall and skinny on the map and the further north the more affluent the neighborhood. Bachelor parties are going to run through the Entertainment District of Old Town, so being close to there for easy Ubers is a must! If your crew desires a big house with a pool you may have to go 10-20 mins outside of this area to the Northeast in neighborhoods such as Scottsdale Highlands or Scottsdale Monterey
